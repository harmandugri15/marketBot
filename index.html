🎯 DEP / 🌊 VT
The foundational base strategies.
1
DEP (Sniper)
Look for an explosion: price up 4% on massive volume. Wait for dead volume and a bounce off the 10 EMA.
2
VT (Momentum)
Price must be trading above the 20 EMA and Daily Pivot. Enter when MACD Histogram rises.
Exit Rules (Universal)
SL
Stop Loss Hit
Price touches your stop loss level. Exit immediately. Take the small loss. Never move the SL further away.
2R
Move SL to Break Even
When the trade is up 2× your initial risk, move the stop loss to your entry price. Now the worst outcome is 0% — you can't lose on this trade.
3R
Sell Half the Position
When up 3× initial risk, sell 50% of your shares. This locks in profits while letting the rest run.
EMA
Trail on 20 EMA
For the remaining 50%, use the 20-day EMA as a trailing stop. When price closes below the 20 EMA, sell.
